# Alt text — Kernel and Authority Stack

A dark landscape diagram presents Graph Climbing as a four-part system. On the left, a large mint Claim Graph panel is labeled as the permanent product authority for intent, atomic claims, dependencies, decisions, and official progress. In the center, a circular Climber runs derive, select, build, verify, and reconcile. On the right, an amber Evidence panel holds snapshot-bound tests, runtime observations, reviews, and release artifacts. A purple optional Execution Graph and Ledger panel below it holds verticals, owners, gates, and debt. Solid arrows form the claim-frontier-to-climber-to-evidence-to-claim-graph loop. Dashed arrows connect the optional execution layer to the climber. A footer states that one authority exists per concern and generated views are not authorities.

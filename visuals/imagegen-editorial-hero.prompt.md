# Imagegen editorial hero disposition

Status: rejected as the publication source; deterministic SVG fallback adopted.

The generated candidate successfully showed two separate climbs, a shared foundation, and returning evidence trails. It was not adopted because its block peaks read as literal mountains and staircases, violating the requested anti-cliché constraint. The generated file remains in the tool's default output store and is not referenced by the project.

## Prompt

```text
Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: text-free editorial hero for a technical essay about Graph Climbing
Primary request: Create a sophisticated abstract landscape that conveys durable product truth, bounded local climbs, and evidence returning to a shared source of truth without becoming a literal diagram.
Scene/backdrop: deep midnight navy field with layered architectural terrain; a stable low horizontal lattice forms the foundation, two clearly separated luminous vertical paths rise from reachable points, and fine amber particles curve back toward the foundation as reconciled evidence.
Style/medium: premium editorial 3D illustration, restrained geometric forms, subtle depth, crisp matte materials, publication-grade, not sci-fi UI.
Composition/framing: wide 16:9 landscape, strong central-to-right visual movement, generous negative space at upper left, readable as a hero at desktop and mobile crop.
Lighting/mood: calm, rigorous, quietly ambitious; soft cyan, cobalt, violet, and amber accents on a near-black background.
Constraints: absolutely no words, letters, numbers, labels, arrows, logos, watermarks, interface panels, flowchart boxes, or pseudo-text. The two rising paths must remain spatially separate and return visually to one stable foundation. No people.
Avoid: literal mountain, staircase cliché, corporate stock art, neon overload, tangled lines, impossible intersections, circuit-board aesthetic, diagrams, infographics.
```

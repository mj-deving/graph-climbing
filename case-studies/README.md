# Case-study map

The DACS Forge run produces several related artifacts. They have different scopes and completion conditions.

## Published now

- [DACS Forge in progress](dacs-agent-template.md) is an interim Graph Climbing field report. It covers the work graph, steering, governance detour, scope recovery, review provenance, and the active frontier through 2026-07-21.
- [From Vibe Coding to Hardened Software](autoreview-semantic-hardening.md) is a short reusable account of the inner Autoreview hardening loop, grounded in the bilateral Vet vertical.

## After the build

- The final Graph Climbing case study will cover the complete macro loop from initial claim graph through final reconciliation and outcome.
- The DACS Forge engineering case study will cover the technical system itself: DACS verification, protocol and authority boundaries, signed artifacts, lifecycle invariants, persistence, crash safety, concurrency, interfaces, conformance, container release, and measured qualification.

The two final case studies share source evidence but make different claims. Process evidence does not substitute for product engineering evidence, and an impressive product build does not by itself validate the Graph Climbing operating model.
